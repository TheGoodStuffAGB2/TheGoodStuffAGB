1. Open nexus font 
   nexusfont.exe
2. Move the fonts you want into downloads. 
when nexus font is open, look at your library.
3. Right click on sample folder group
4. Add a folder
5. Locate the downloads foler. Make sure your fonts are only in downloads and not in a folder inside downloads
6. Click ok.
7. As long as you have NexusFont open, go on any program and type in the name of your font very precisley.
   Now you have fonts installed!
